    About Us

    Fannan includes everything related to the world of art from marketing and encouraging the Bahraini artist and introducing the community to him and his artworks. With fannan you will do the thing that you think it's impossible to do. You will grow with us and you will become what you want. Everyone will know you through us.

    Services

    Online Courses

    Online courses will be offered by artists carefully selected by fannan studio to help anyone entering the world of art and what is related to the art world.

    Studio programs

    Orientation programs for those who want to enter the world of art and design, so fannan programs aims to help these people reach their goal and achieve it.

    Exhibitions and Annual auction

    Fannan has the advantage of electronic exhibitions and the annual auction, which will allow participants the opportunity to present their work to the world, also everyone will be able to buy works in exhibitions and auctions.
